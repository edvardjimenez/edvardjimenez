Our Rooms

Our rooms, just like each of the hotel’s guest rooms, exude a character that is elegant yet homey, comfortable, and friendly. Ensuring to any of our guest’s to feel the spirit during their stay at the hotel.

Room Features

  • Electric card system
  • Centralized air conditioning with individual temperature control
  • WiFi Access
  • LCD TV with cable channels
  • Telephone units in bedroom and bathroom
  • Mini bar for food preparation
  • In-room safe
  • Shower and bath tub
  • Hot/Cold Water
  • Writing desk and chair
  • Complimentary bottled water (replenished daily)
  • Small fridge

Our Amenities

Heated Indoor Pool

The pool of this hotel allows for the uninterrupted swimming pleasure, rain or shine. The use of the pool is free of charge for registered in-house guests. Charges apply for visitors of in-house guest’s.



Operating Hours is from 6am to 9pm, daily. Entry to pool area only until 7pm, with use of pool until 9pm.



Lobby Cafeteria

A delightfully quaint cafeteria located at the hotl's lobby level, The Lobby Cafeteria is an ideal place in this hotel for meeting friends and colleagues while satisfying cravings for the perfect after meal dessert, midday pick-me-up snack, or daily chocolate fix.



Operating Hours is from 7:00am to 7:00pm during weekdays and 8:00am to 4:00pm during weekends & holidays.
